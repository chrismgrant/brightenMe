Brighten.me is a great way of harnessing the power of networking and crowdsourcing to expand and share your knowledge.

Whether you're a student or official course teaching assistant, you have a certain level of knowledge and proficiency that you can exchange with your peers.

Not every course has flexible office hours or the most approachable TAs. Why not learn from someone who understands your circumstances and can accomodate your busy schedule?

Brighten.me offers this and more by giving peers a community and platform to learn from one another without the stress of paperwork, strict protocols, or lack of availability.
